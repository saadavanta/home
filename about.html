About HomePro Partner

Our Vision

HomePro Partner's long-term vision is to create the most powerful global ecosystem for service providers. We aim to digitize every skill, empower every household with reliable service access, provide steady income to skilled professionals, and build the biggest trusted marketplace for home services.

In the future, we envision HomePro Partner as a platform operating across continents, connecting millions of skilled workers with billions of customers.

Our Journey

From a simple idea to a global movement transforming the home services industry.

2018 - The Beginning

HomePro Partner was founded with a simple mission: to connect skilled professionals with customers who need their services.

2019 - First Launch

We launched our platform in three cities with just 50 service categories and saw immediate adoption.

2020 - Rapid Expansion

Despite global challenges, we expanded to 15 cities and added 30 more service categories.

2021 - Technology Innovation

We launched our mobile apps with features like real-time tracking and advanced matching algorithms.

2022 - International Growth

We expanded to 5 countries, adapting our platform to local markets while maintaining our core values.

2023 - Today

HomePro Partner now operates in 15+ countries with over 500,000 service providers and millions of customers.
