Safety, Trust & Security

Our commitment to keeping you safe

HomePro Partner Follows Strict Safety Rules

Your safety is our top priority. We've implemented comprehensive safety measures to ensure a secure experience for both customers and service providers.

Verified Partner Profiles

All partners must provide valid ID and undergo a thorough verification process before joining our platform. We check credentials, work history, and references to ensure authenticity.

Secure Communication

Customer numbers stay protected through our in-app communication system. You can communicate with service providers without sharing your personal contact information.

Background Checks

Professionals are screened before approval. We conduct background checks to ensure our partners have a clean record and can be trusted in customers' homes.

Rating System

Poor performance lowers visibility; high rating boosts job volume. Our transparent rating system helps maintain quality and accountability across the platform.

Data Protection

Customer and partner data is fully encrypted. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal and financial information.

24/7 Support

Our support team is available around the clock to address any safety concerns or issues that may arise during service delivery.

Safety Guidelines

Tips for a safe service experience

For Customers

  • Always book through the HomePro app to ensure your service is covered by our safety measures
  • Check the provider's rating and reviews before booking
  • Verify the provider's identity when they arrive
  • If possible, have someone else at home during the service
  • Keep valuables and sensitive documents out of sight
  • Trust your instincts - if something doesn't feel right, contact our support team
  • Rate your service provider honestly after the job is completed

For Service Providers

  • Always arrive on time and communicate any delays
  • Wear your HomePro Partner ID badge at all times during service
  • Respect the customer's property and privacy
  • Follow professional standards and safety protocols for your service
  • Keep your tools and equipment organized and secure
  • Communicate clearly about the service process and any additional costs
  • Leave the work area clean and tidy after completing the service

Emergency Procedures

What to do in case of an emergency

1

Ensure Immediate Safety

If there's an immediate threat to safety, remove yourself from the situation first. Your safety is the top priority.

2

Contact Emergency Services

If there's a medical emergency, fire, or criminal activity, call local emergency services immediately.

3

Report to HomePro

After ensuring immediate safety, report the incident to HomePro Partner through the app or our emergency hotline.

4

Document Everything

If it's safe to do so, take photos or videos of the situation and keep any relevant evidence.

5

Cooperate with Investigation

Provide all necessary information to assist with the investigation and resolution of the incident.
