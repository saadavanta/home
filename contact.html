Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about HomePro Partner.

How do I become a partner?

Download our app, register with your details, select your service category, and complete the verification process. Once approved, you'll start receiving job requests.

How much does it cost to join?

Joining HomePro Partner is completely free. We only charge a small commission on completed jobs, which is deducted from your earnings.

How quickly do I get paid?

Earnings are added to your wallet immediately after customer confirmation. Withdrawals to mobile wallets are instant, while bank transfers may take 1-3 business days.

Is my information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and financial information. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.
