How It Works

Join HomePro Partner in 8 simple steps and start earning today

Our Simple Process

We've streamlined the entire process to make it easy for skilled professionals to join our platform and start earning immediately.

Download App

Install HomePro Partner from Play Store / App Store. Our app is available for both Android and iOS devices.

Register

Add your name, phone number, service category, CNIC / ID, address, and experience. The more information you provide, the better we can match you with relevant jobs.

Verification

Our team verifies your identity and service category. This typically takes 24-48 hours. You'll receive a notification once verification is complete.

Profile Activation

Once approved, your profile becomes visible to customers. Make sure your profile is complete and professional to attract more customers.

Receive Job Requests

You'll receive job notifications based on your location, service category, and availability. Each request includes customer details, location, service requirements, and budget.

Accept & Contact

Once you accept a job, you can contact the customer directly through our app to discuss details, schedule a time, and answer any questions.

Complete the Job

Deliver excellent service to the customer. Be punctual, professional, and thorough. Quality service leads to positive reviews and more job opportunities.

Earn Money

After completing the job, mark it as completed in the app. Your earnings will be added to your wallet after customer confirmation. You can withdraw your earnings anytime.

App Features

Powerful tools to manage your business efficiently and grow your earnings.

Job Notifications

Receive instant notifications when new jobs matching your skills and location are posted. Never miss an opportunity.

Schedule Management

Organize your jobs, set your availability, and manage your calendar all within the app.

Route Optimization

Get the most efficient routes between jobs to save time and reduce travel expenses.

In-App Communication

Communicate with customers directly through the app. Your personal number stays protected.

Earnings Dashboard

Track your earnings, view payment history, and manage your wallet all in one place.

Rating System

Build your reputation with customer ratings and reviews. Higher ratings lead to more job opportunities.
