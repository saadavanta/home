Payment & Earnings System

How partners get paid on HomePro Partner

How Partners Get Paid

Payments are deposited into partner wallets and can be withdrawn anytime. We've designed our payment system to be simple, transparent, and efficient.

Complete the Job

After providing the service, mark the job as completed in the app

Customer Confirmation

Customer confirms the job is completed to their satisfaction

Earnings Added

Your earnings are added to your wallet after deducting the platform fee

Withdraw Funds

Withdraw your earnings anytime using your preferred payment method

What's Included in Your Earnings

Your total earnings include:

Service Cost

The base price for the service provided as agreed with the customer

Additional Items

Cost of any materials or parts provided for the service

Emergency Charges

Additional fees for emergency or after-hours services (if applicable)

Hourly or Fixed Pricing

Based on the job type and pricing structure agreed with the customer

Withdrawal Methods

Choose from multiple convenient withdrawal options:

Bank Transfer

Direct transfer to your bank account. Usually takes 1-3 business days.

JazzCash / Easypaisa

Instant transfer to your mobile wallet account.

Local Wallet

Transfer to other local wallet options available in your region.

Payment FAQ

How quickly do I receive my earnings?

Earnings are added to your wallet immediately after the customer confirms job completion. Withdrawals to mobile wallets are typically instant, while bank transfers may take 1-3 business days depending on your bank.

What is the platform commission?

HomePro Partner charges a small commission on completed jobs, which varies by service category and region. The exact commission rate is displayed before you accept a job, so there are no surprises.

Are there any withdrawal fees?

Withdrawal fees vary by method and region. Mobile wallet withdrawals are typically free or have minimal fees, while bank transfers may have a small processing fee. All fees are clearly displayed before you confirm a withdrawal.

What if a customer disputes a payment?

If a customer disputes a payment, the amount is temporarily held while our dispute resolution team investigates. We review evidence from both parties and make a fair decision based on the HomePro Protect program guidelines.
